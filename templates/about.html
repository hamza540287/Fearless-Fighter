History of Cricket

From village fields in England to packed international stadiums, cricket has become one of the world's most loved sports.

The Journey of Cricket

Cricket originated in England during the 16th century. It gradually evolved into an international sport and today it is played professionally in more than one hundred countries.

The first officially recognized Test Match was played in 1877 between England and Australia. Since then, One Day Internationals and T20 cricket have transformed the game, making it faster and even more exciting.
